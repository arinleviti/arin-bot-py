# Hero Burger

## The Project
Hero Burger is an ongoing freelance client — a gourmet burger restaurant whose web presence Arin maintains and extends. The project involved combining technical precision with creative brand interpretation, working closely with the client and their social media agency to ensure every visual and interaction choice felt authentic to Hero Burger's identity.

## What Was Built
Arin used Framer Motion extensively to bring the site to life with smooth, interactive animations that make browsing feel dynamic without overwhelming the user. State management ensures menu filters and dynamic content respond seamlessly. He integrated the client's branding guidelines and "color bible" into the design, creating a digital presence that mirrors the in-person experience. Additional features include a birthday contest landing page with a custom CSS envelope animation component, a seasonal opening-hours component with automatic logic for summer lunch hour changes, and blog post tooling using Prisma and Cloudinary.

## Stack
The Hero Burger site is built with Next.js, React, Framer Motion, Prisma, and Cloudinary, with a focus on performance, brand consistency, and maintainability.
