# Retro Horror Hub

## What it is
Retro Horror Hub (RHH) is Arin's personal passion project — a horror film collectibles site built with Next.js, Supabase, and Prisma, deployed on Netlify.

## Technical highlights
A custom eBay curation algorithm filters and scores listings, handling sequel detection and franchise grouping. Integrations with TMDB, Discogs, YouTube, Spotify, and Reddit enrich each movie page.

## Growth and SEO
Outreach to horror blogs and collector communities, plus Instagram Reels, are the main growth levers for a niche site like this.
