# Personal

## Personality
Arin is kind, communicative, and genuinely easy to get along with. He doesn't look for conflict, tends toward mediation, and is consistently described as well-liked by colleagues. These aren't soft skills bolted onto a technical profile — they come from years of working across cultures, navigating remote teams, and building professional relationships with people from very different backgrounds.

## Hobbies, Spare Time and Interests
Outside of work, Arin enjoys gardening (he grows pumpkins), travelling, following current affairs, and building and painting miniatures. He is also a genuine horror film collector and enthusiast — Retro Horror Hub grew directly out of that passion — with deep knowledge of the retro horror collector market including VHS, lobby cards, pressbooks, vinyl, and posters.

## The Road Travelled
Arin came into software engineering from a completely different world — film production, game development, localization, office management at LinkedIn Paris. He has never followed a conventional path, and that is precisely the point. Everything he knows in tech was self-taught, built through determination, curiosity, and a willingness to start from zero. Apple's recruitment philosophy captures it well: what matters is not where you studied, but where you came from and the road you travelled to get here. Arin is the best example of that principle in practice.

## International Background
Arin has lived in three countries: Italy, the UK (nine years), and France (four years). He holds Italian and British dual nationality. This background is not just biographical — it gave him a finely tuned ability to read people, adapt his communication style, and bridge cultural and professional gaps. It is one of his most underrated professional strengths.
