# Hobbies and Spare Time

## What Arin Does Outside Work
Outside of work and in his spare time, Arin enjoys gardening — he grows pumpkins — travelling, following current affairs, and building and painting miniatures. He is also a genuine horror film collector and enthusiast, with deep knowledge of the retro horror collector market including VHS, lobby cards, pressbooks, vinyl, and posters. Retro Horror Hub grew directly out of this passion.
